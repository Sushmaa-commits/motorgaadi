Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is MOTOR GADI?

    MOTOR GADI is an online taxi hire/booking platform that has different services to suite your taste, our services will be attached to this document.

  • How can we get MOTOR GADI?

    You can get MOTOR GADI Passenger mobile apps on Play store or Apple store, and you can also access our web App by visiting MOTOR GADI.com on you web browser. We suggest you to get MOTOR GADI Passenger app mobile app from Play Store or Apple Store.

  • What are the benefits Users (Passenger/Riders) get by using MOTOR GADI platform to book taxi?

    Users (Passengers/Riders) get a lot of benefits ranging from thier Comfortable pick up from your door step, flexible payment process(cash/esewa/wallet), affordable fare (as per Nepal Bureau of Standards and Metrology rate), you get to know you estimated price before arrival of cab. You will pay as you go. No extra or hidden charges.

  • What are drivers Benefit if working with MOTOR GADI platform?

    Drivers are our assets, Drivers are charged less percentage, Drivers get's rating after a trip, there is a flexible percentage range for drivers and percentage rise up if drivers reach a certain amount of rating on the apps. High rated drivers gets more benefits like free/subsidized data pack from the company, Star tag in profile gets more rides and have to pay less commission to MOTOR GADI.

  • What benefit does car owners have?

    Car owners get safe drivers to work in their cars and maintenance of the car shall be given proper attention on terms, there is a good percentage as well that goes to every car owners per bookings.

  • What benefit are in for the Franchisee?

    For Firms or Individual getting Franchise with us, we've figured out a profiting policy and flexible procedures, which you make an order for through mail by sending a mail to motorgadi3@gmail.com after which the Policy and procedures will be directed to you through mail.

  • What are the procedures to register as MOTOR GADI driver?

    To Register as a MOTOR GADI Driver, You'll need to have a valid Driver's License, and you'll enroll for a Driver test and Orientation with MOTOR GADI Driver Institution, our Driving Instructor will orient you on how to interact on our mobile apps platform, will also orient you on how to interact with Riders and what to do if issues occurs/happens on road, after which you've passed MOTOR GADI Drivers Orientation.

  • What are the type of Taxi accepted to work with MOTOR GADI?

    The requirements for car approval are:-
    (i) The cars must not be older than 12years of Manufacturing date.
    (ii) Only Four doors Sedan and Multi-Purpose Vehicle (MPV) no other types of cars are accepted except for those mentioned for now.
    (iii) All taxi must have all legal documents to run on Kathmandu Valley registered as Commercial Taxi, these documents include Vehicle license, Certificate of Road Worthiness, Driver's License, Insurance Certificate, Proof of Ownership, Taxi Registration.
    (iv) Vehicle Inspection approval, these will check all the vehicle features and their working conditions if it's suitable to work with MOTOR GADI, vehicles might be directed to an inspection site if required. Further information will be given after registration.
    (v) All MOTOR GADI taxi must not have any advertisement at the time of registration.
