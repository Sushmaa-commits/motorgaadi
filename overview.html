How does MotorGadi Apps Works

Driver overview


01. Need A Car

You can sign up to drive, even if you don’t have a car right now. In the meantime, we’ll give you access to rental and leasing deals. Their website is filled with deals on rental and financing options that you can’t find anywhere else.

02. Driver App

The new Driver app helps you earn smarter with real-time information. Easier to use and more reliable, the app now supports you—like a partner—at every turn.

03. Rewards

When you drive with MOTOR GADI, you become an MOTOR GADI partner. We work hard to make sure this partnership is worth your while, and one way we do this is by making sure we secure the best third-party deals and discounts for you.

04. . Driver safety

Drive with confidence, you deserve to be able to go wherever the opportunity is. Get there with support on the road and technology that helps protect you and those around you.

The MOTOR GADI app matches you with a nearby driver to take you wherever you want to go.

Rider Overview

Requesting a ride

01. Download

Get the free MOTOR GADI app from the App Store or Google Play on your smartphone. Open the app to create your account.

02. Request

Enter your destination and choose a ride option. You’ll always see the price up front.

03. Ride

You'll see your driver's picture and vehicle details, and can track their arrival on the map.
